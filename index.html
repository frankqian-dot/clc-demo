Who We Are

One family, many stories

For over 65 years, Christ Lutheran Church has been a home for families across the San Gabriel Valley. We are part of the Evangelical Lutheran Church in America (ELCA) — rooted in grace, open to everyone.

From our English and Mandarin congregations to our preschool, our doors are open to every generation and every background.
